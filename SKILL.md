# Langrensha (狼人杀) - AI Werewolf Tactics

## Overview

Comprehensive tactical guide for AI werewolf (狼人杀) games, integrating classical Chinese strategic wisdom:
- **《孙子兵法》** (Sun Tzu's Art of War)
- **《鬼谷子》** (Guiguzi's strategic philosophy)  
- **《三十六计》** (Thirty-Six Stratagems)

This skill provides role-specific tactics, gameplay workflows, and psychological warfare techniques for asynchronous text-based werewolf games.

## Core Philosophy: 捭阖之道 (Bai He Zhi Dao)

> "捭之者，开也，言也，阳也；阖之者，闭也，默也，阴也。"

**The Way of Opening and Closing:**
- **捭 (Bai/Open)**: Speak actively, guide topics, establish persona
- **阖 (He/Close)**: Stay silent, observe, hide identity
- **Bai He You Du**: Know when to speak and when to stay silent

## Role Tactics Overview

### 🐺 Werewolf (狼人)
**Core Tactic**: "能而示之不能，用而示之不用" (Appear weak when strong)

**Key Strategies**:
- **瞒天过海**: Disguise as a good person
- **借刀杀人**: Turn villagers against each other
- **苦肉计**: Get suspected then "cleared" to build trust
- **反间计**: Create internal conflict among villagers

### 🔮 Prophet/Seer (预言家)
**Core Tactic**: "知彼知己者，百战不殆" (Know yourself and your enemy)

**Key Strategies**:
- **抛砖引玉**: Reveal small info to elicit reactions
- **打草惊蛇**: Fake checks to see who panics
- **擒贼擒王**: Check the most active players first

### 🛡️ Guard (守卫)
**Core Tactic**: "守而必固者，守其所不攻也" (Defend where they won't attack)

**Key Strategies**:
- **抵巇之术**: Block vulnerabilities before attacks
- **预判暗杀**: Predict who wolves will target

### 🧪 Witch (女巫)
**Core Tactic**: "奇正之变，不可胜穷也" (Endless variations of orthodox and unorthodox)

**Key Strategies**:
- **釜底抽薪**: Poison key werewolves at critical moments
- **欲擒故纵**: Delay using potions to observe the situation

### 🌾 Villager (村民)
**Core Tactic**: "反以观往，覆以验来" (Observe the past to verify the future)

**Key Strategies**:
- **多听少说**: Listen more, speak less
- **投石问路**: Use probing questions to test others
- **浑水摸鱼**: Spot opportunities in chaos

## Game Flow Tactics

### Phase 1: Opening (开局)
Use **飞箝之术** (Flying and Binding):
> "大家都是实事求是的乖宝宝，请大家说出你们的角色~ 😊"

- **飞**: Use praise ("乖宝宝") to lower defenses
- **箝**: Guide them to reveal information
- **Key Rule**: Ask others, but never reveal your own role

### Phase 2: Night (夜晚)
**Werewolf**: Attack unprepared targets (攻其无备)
**Prophet**: Check based on threat assessment
**Guard**: Predict and protect high-value targets

### Phase 3: Day Discussion (白天讨论)
The core battlefield of psychological warfare!

**Werewolf Tactics**:
- Establish "rational" persona through analysis
- Use **瞒天过海** to appear innocent
- Deploy **第一回合话术** (First Round Script): "乖宝宝" approach

**Villager Tactics**:
- Use **反应之术**: Listen first, analyze reactions
- Watch for coordination patterns (werewolves work together)
- Apply **捭阖观察法** track who speaks vs. who observes

### Phase 4: Voting (投票)
**《孙子兵法》· 决战之道**:
1. Calculate votes: How many can I influence?
2. Predict outcomes: What happens if I vote X?
3. Risk assessment: What if I'm wrong?
